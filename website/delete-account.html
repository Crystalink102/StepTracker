Delete Your Account

You can delete your account and all associated data at any time.

Option 1: Delete from the App

The fastest way to delete your account is directly within the app:

  1. Open 5tepTracker
  2. Go to Settings (gear icon)
  3. Tap Account
  4. Tap Delete Account
  5. Confirm the deletion

Your account and all data will be permanently deleted immediately.

Option 2: Request Deletion by Email

If you can no longer access the app, you can request account deletion by emailing us:

Request Account Deletion

Include the email address or phone number associated with your account. We will process your request and delete all data within 7 business days.

What Data Gets Deleted

When you delete your account, the following data is permanently removed:

  • Profile information
  • Step history
  • Activity records & routes
  • Achievements & XP
  • Friends & social data
  • Challenge participation
  • Personal bests
  • Notification preferences

This action is permanent and cannot be undone. There is no recovery period.
