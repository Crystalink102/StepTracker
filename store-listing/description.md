## Full Description (4000 char max)

5tepTracker turns your daily steps and runs into a game you actually want to play. Earn XP for every step, level up, maintain streaks, and compete with friends on the leaderboard.

**STEP TRACKING**
Accurate step counting powered by Google Health Connect. Your steps sync automatically throughout the day — no need to keep the app open. Set a daily step goal and watch your progress in real time.

**GPS RUN & WALK TRACKING**
Start a run or walk with one tap. Get live GPS tracking with pace, distance, and route mapping. Review your routes and stats after every activity. Train with interval workouts using built-in templates like 5K Speedwork, HIIT Sprint, and Tempo Intervals.

**XP & LEVELING SYSTEM**
Every step earns experience points. Level up as you move and celebrate milestones. The more active you are, the faster you progress. It's fitness meets RPG.

**STREAKS & GOALS**
Build daily step streaks and running streaks. Use streak freezes on rest days to protect your progress. Set custom daily goals and track your consistency over time.

**FRIENDS & LEADERBOARDS**
Add friends, see their daily steps, and compete on weekly and all-time leaderboards. Cheer each other on through the social feed with likes and comments.

**CHALLENGES**
Create step or distance challenges and invite friends. Race to the finish line together and see who comes out on top.

**ACHIEVEMENTS**
Unlock badges for hitting milestones — step goals, streak records, challenge completions, personal bests, and more. Collect them all.

**DETAILED STATS**
View weekly summaries, training load, pace trends, and distance breakdowns. Track personal bests across standard distances from 100m to marathon.

**SECURITY**
Your account is protected with optional two-factor authentication (TOTP). Your data is stored securely with row-level security — only you can see your data, and only friends can see your steps.

Download 5tepTracker and start leveling up today.
