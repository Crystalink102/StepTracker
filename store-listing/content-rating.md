# IARC Content Rating Questionnaire - 5tepTracker

Answers for the Google Play content rating questionnaire.

---

## Violence
**Does the app contain violence?** No

## Sexual Content
**Does the app contain sexual content or nudity?** No

## Language
**Does the app contain profanity or crude humor?** No

## Controlled Substances
**Does the app reference or promote drugs, alcohol, or tobacco?** No

## Gambling
**Does the app contain simulated gambling?** No
(Note: The XP/leveling system is progression-based, NOT gambling — there are no random rewards, loot boxes, or paid randomized outcomes)

## User-Generated Content
**Can users communicate or share content with each other?** Yes
- Users can post activity updates to a social feed
- Users can like and comment on friends' activities
- Users have display names and profile photos visible to friends

**Is there moderation?**
- Content is limited to fitness activities (auto-generated)
- User-generated text is limited to comments and display names
- Users can only interact with accepted friends (not open/public)

## Personal Information
**Does the app collect personal data?** Yes
- Email, display name, health/fitness data, location (during active tracking)

## Location
**Does the app share the user's location with other users?** No
- GPS routes are private and never shared with other users

## Ads
**Does the app contain ads?** No

---

## Expected Rating
Based on these answers, expect a rating of **PEGI 3 / Everyone** (no objectionable content).
